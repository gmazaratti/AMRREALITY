The Vision

Our Mission

01

Heritage Meets Reality

AMR Reality was born from a lineage of ambition. What started as AMR — a name built across grocery, aviation, and enterprise — evolved into something personal. Reality by Aish is the next chapter: a fashion house rooted in legacy but defined by a new generation's creative vision.

02

Designed Without Compromise

Every piece we create is intentional. We reject mass production and disposable trends. Our collections are limited, our materials are deliberate, and every silhouette is engineered to feel as precise as it looks. This is clothing built with the same discipline that built an empire.

03

Montreal to the World

Rooted in Montreal, designed for everywhere. Our coordinates aren't decorative — they're a statement of origin. We carry the grit, the culture, and the raw energy of our city into every drop. AMR Reality is where streetwear meets structure, where local identity becomes global language.

04

The Reality Standard

We don't follow seasons. We follow conviction. Each collection drops when it's ready — not when the calendar says so. Reality by Aish exists for those who move with intention, who wear what they believe in, and who understand that real luxury is built, never borrowed.

05

Building Beyond Fashion

This isn't just about clothing. 15% of proceeds from every sale will go directly towards building a school in Jaffna, northern Sri Lanka. Fashion gave us a platform — and we intend to use it. AMR Reality exists to create opportunity, not just garments. When you wear Reality by Aish, you're part of something that reaches far beyond the fabric.
